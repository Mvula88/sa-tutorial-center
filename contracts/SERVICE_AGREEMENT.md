### 5.1 Cancellation by Client
The Client may cancel this agreement at any time by providing **30 days written notice** via email or WhatsApp.

### 5.2 Cancellation by Service Provider
The Service Provider may terminate this agreement with 30 days notice if:
- The Client fails to pay fees for more than 60 days
- The Client violates the terms of this agreement
- The Client uses the system for illegal activities

### 5.3 What Happens to Your Data Upon Termination
Upon termination of this agreement:

1. **Data Export:** The Client will have **30 days** to request an export of all their data. The Service Provider will provide data in a standard format (Excel/CSV) at no additional charge.

2. **Data Retention:** After the 30-day period, the Service Provider will securely delete all Client data from the system, unless legally required to retain it.

3. **No Data Hostage:** The Service Provider will never withhold Client data as leverage for unpaid fees. Data export will be provided regardless of payment disputes.

4. **Website:** If the Client has a website and chooses not to continue, domain transfer instructions will be provided if the Client wishes to keep their domain name.

### 5.4 Refunds
- Setup fees are non-refundable
- Monthly fees paid in advance will not be refunded upon cancellation
- Annual website renewal fees are non-refundable
